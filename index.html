Pokemon

Pokemon is an extremely popular game that came out in 1996. It is a series of video games and a tv show. The series mascot is the pokemon pikachu.


Each generation has its own fire, water, and grass type starter pokemon.

Fire pokemon are super effective against grass pokemon but lose to water pokemon

Water pokemon are super effective against fire pokemon but lose to grass pokemon

Grass pokemon are super effective against water pokemon but lose to fire pokemon
